# Roguehood Story

In the heart of Sherwood Forest, where the moon shines bright and the leaves whisper secrets, lives Rogue—the legendary outlaw fox. He's not here to steal from the rich or give to the poor (though he might steal a meme or two). He's here to build a community of rogues, misfits, and dreamers. Together, we're making history—one meme at a time.
